Vegetable Fried Rice

Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add vegetables and fry
  3. Add rice and sauces
  4. Mix well and cook
